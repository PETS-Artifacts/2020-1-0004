
At Casa Santo Domingo, we sincerely value your privacy. We want you to be confident in using this website to make online reservations and explore our gorgeous and historical hotel.
We are very aware of your concerns about the privacy of your information and have no desire or intent to infringe on your privacy. We will not provide your personal information to anyone else.
When you provide your personal information such as name, address, email address and telephone number, we will not give or sell this information to any outside company for any use. This information will be kept confidential, and will be used only to support your customer relationship with us. We have appropriate security measures in place to protect against the loss, misuse or alteration of information we have collected from you through our site.
