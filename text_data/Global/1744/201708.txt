
Privacy & Cookie PolicyThe BrainBashers website uses cookies, more information is contained below. Continued use of BrainBashers indicates that you have read, understood, and agreed with the information provided. Use of any of our websites is also subject to our terms and conditions.

  We never send spam emails, make spam telephone calls, send spam postal mail, etc.
  Please feel free to contact us if you have any comments.

We may modify or revise the privacy policy at any time by updating this web page. You should therefore visit this page periodically to review the details.
The BrainBashers website uses third party vendors, including Google, for some of its adverts, and as a third-party vendor, they use cookies to serve ads based on a user's previous visits to the BrainBashers website or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to the BrainBashers website and/or other sites on the internet. You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
The BrainBashers website also uses other third-party advertising networks certified by Google (a list of which can be seen here https://www.google.com/adsense/support/bin/answer.py?answer=94149). You may visit any of those websites to opt out of the cookies used in personalised advertising, if the vendor or ad network offers this capability. Alternatively, you can opt out of some third-party vendors' use of cookies for personalised advertising by visiting aboutads.info.
Google Analytics is used to analyse our traffic.
Google have provided a page entitled "How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps" that explains how they use the data that they collect.
We also occasionally use Google's reCAPTCHA to verify that our users are not robots. The reCAPTCHA box ('I am not a robot') has a clickable link to the relevant privacy policy.
In addition, the BrainBashers website itself uses cookies when you have:
   Closed the banner across the top advising you about our use of cookies.
   Recorded an 'A - Z' time (or reset your personal best).
   Recorded a '1 - 100' time.
   Saved a Sudoku.
   Played 'Daily Web Words'.
   Played 'Guess The Number'.
   Played 'PicturePuzzle'.
   Played 'Twenty Levels'.

We use server-side ASP session variables to: facilitate various puzzles and games, to count usage, etc. Usually these are automatically destroyed when your session expires.
As with most other websites, we collect and use the server's logging information. This information includes details such as your IP (internet protocol) address, the browser you used to visit our site (such as Internet Explorer or Firefox), the time you visited our site and which pages you visited throughout our site, etc.
Any information submitted to us via the 'contact us' form may be quoted to a third party, and may also be quoted in a public forum (your name and other identifying information, including your email, will remain private). In addition, information received after an exchange of emails initiated by the 'contact us' form may also be used in this way. Use of the 'contact us' system indicates your permission.
The names, countries and answers of competition entrants may be posted to the website or public newsgroups at our discretion. Search engines may then index the site and your name appear in their listings. Entry into a contest indicates your permission to use your details in this way.
We never sell any of your personal information to third parties. However we will disclose any required information if legally obligated.
