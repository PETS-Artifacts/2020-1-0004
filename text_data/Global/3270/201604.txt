Patient Privacy Practices for Methodist Healthcare

Published On  
07/11/2011





We take your privacy very seriously and uphold HIPAA laws. We will not use or disclose your medical information without your authorization, except as described in the Notice of Privacy Practices listed below.
Notices of Privacy Practices
All hospitals and facilities follow the Methodist Healthcare Hospital Notice of Privacy Practices unless otherwise listed. 

Methodist Healthcare Facilities (PDF)
Aviso Conjunto de Prácticas de Privacidad (PDF)
