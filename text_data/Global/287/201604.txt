
Fordham University is committed to protecting your privacy. Information you provide to us through a Fordham University website such as email address, name, or phone number will not be sold or traded to other outside organizations. Fordham University may use this information to inform you of changes to our websites or to provide you other relevant news and information. At your request, Fordham University will remove any contact information you provided from its files.
Information gathered automatically
Unless you take steps to browse the Internet anonymously, Fordham University, like most institutions and organizations, tracks web browsing patterns to evaluate how our sites are being used. This information is collected through the use of "cookies," or other web tracking tools, which are text files placed on your computer. You may set your browser to prevent the use of cookies; however, doing so may prevent you from using certain functions of our websites. The generic information we collect is based on IP address, which is the location of a computer or network, and is not linked to your specific email address in any of the reports or other material compiled by Fordham University. Fordham University does use server logs to collect information concerning the Internet connection and general information about visits to our websites. This data may be acquired, recorded and analyzed for the purposes of improving the content of the website, analyzing trends, investigating security concerns, and identifying system performance or problem areas. Fordham University will not associate your IP address and web usage data with any other information held by Fordham University that can specifically identify you unless required to do so by law.
Fordham University may, from time to time, use web analytical services such as the service currently provided to the University by Google, Inc. Google Analytics and other similar services use cookies to help us analyze how users use our sites. The information generated by the cookie about your use of the website includes your IP address, and this information will be transmitted to and stored by the services.
