
8x8’s International Privacy Policies
8x8, Inc. is committed to respecting our customers’ and prospective customers’ privacy. 8x8 has a privacy policy covering our current customers and one for prospective customers, which are linked below. Our privacy policies describe the information we collect and how we use and share that information.
In addition, 8x8, Inc. declares its commitment to the European Union-United States Privacy Shield Framework, as set forth by the U.S. Department of Commerce regarding the collection, use, and retention of personal information from European Economic Area member countries.  8x8, Inc. is also committed to comply with the United States-Swiss Safe Harbor framework governing the collection, use, and retention of personal information from Switzerland.  8x8, Inc. has certified that it adheres to the Privacy Shield Principles of notice, choice, accountability for onward transfer, security, data integrity and purpose limitation, access, recourse, enforcement, and liability. To learn more about the Privacy Shield program, to view 8x8 Inc.’s certification page, and to view a list of businesses that have self-certified compliance with the Privacy Shield, please visit https://www.privacyshield.gov/welcome.
Thank you for taking the time to review 8x8’s privacy policies.
