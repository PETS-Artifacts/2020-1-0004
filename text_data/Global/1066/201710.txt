
Amazon Watch is committed to preserving your privacy and safeguarding your information online. The following statement describes the general information-gathering and -usage practices of our website.
Amazon Watch does not sell or trade names or other personal data that we collect on our website.
Credit card donations through our web site are processed by Authorize.Net. Amazon Watch never receives or stores your credit card information in any format, so there is no risk that it might be hacked or disclosed. We keep a record of your donation and contact information (name, address, etc.) in our database of supporters. 
Any bulk mailings we send are clearly identified as being from Amazon Watch and will always include options to unsubscribe and contact us.
We work hard to ensure that your experience on our site is a positive one. If you have any questions about this privacy policy or your dealings with our site, please contact us.
