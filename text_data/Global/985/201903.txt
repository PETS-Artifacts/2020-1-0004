

How to make a complaint
Please  tell us if  you’re not happy  with how we’ve handled your personal  information. For  more information  about our complaint process, please  visit How to Make a Complaint. You are also entitled to make a complaint to the Fundraising Regulator and the Information Commissioner’s Office.
Privacy notice review
We    regularly review this Privacy Notice. If we make changes they’ll be    updated here and we will place a prominent notice on our website.
This privacy notice was prepared to be as concise as possible. It does not    provide exhaustive detail of all aspects of the RSPCA’s collection  and   use of your personal information. However, we are happy to provide  any   additional information or explanation needed. Any requests for  this   should be sent to the address below.
