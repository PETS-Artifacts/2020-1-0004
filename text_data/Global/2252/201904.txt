This means we can recommend more relevant experiences for you next time you choose to use Responsible Travel. \nBecause you don\'t make any bookings directly with Responsible Travel we don\'t have access to any of your payment or passport information.\n\nSo to clarify, the only personal contact information we hold about you is your name and email address, plus we remember some details of your past enquiries and bookings.
