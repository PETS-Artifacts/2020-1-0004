World War I Document Archive:Privacy policy

From World War I Document Archive


					Jump to:					navigation, 					search

Contributors to the World War I Document Archive are welcome.
However, a sign-in and password are necessary in order to add and edit material; thus your contributions will be evaluated for retention and you will not be anonymous.  
In all other ways, your privacy is sancrosanct.
