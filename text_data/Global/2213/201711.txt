Message Boards

Visitors wishing to use Creative Uncut's message boards, or forums, are advised to be over the age of 13. Users will be asked to provide their email address upon registering for an account. This is used to confirm your registration and will not be shared with a third party. Users will have the option of filling in additional personal details in the 
submission form and in their public profile. Please be aware that these details are viewable by anyone. Caution should be taken when releasing any personal details via the message boards. Creative Uncut cannot be held responsible for any information posted publicly by users.

Fan Art Submissions

This website has, at times, accepted fan artwork submitted by visitors. These artists provide the username of their choice along with their fan art to be posted on the Creative Uncut website. By default, an artist's email address and real name will not be listed. 
However, if you express desire to have this information publicly listed, we will do so at your request. Should you request to have your email or real name listed, but later change your mind, we will remove it at your request. When submitting a fan art, your email and name will not be shared with a third party.
