How is your personally identifiable information protected? 
We are committed to providing secure online banking services using the Internet. Encryption is used to protect the transmission of your personally identifiable information when completing online application forms. Our Internet servers are protected by firewalls and intrusion detection systems. Access to information on these servers is restricted to authorised personnel only. We have also employed the services of independent security experts to test and advise us on the security of our systems and to ensure that our systems' security complies with internationally accepted standards. 
To ensure that you benefit from our security we advise you to read the security tips that we post on our website from time to time.
