When you use the Website, EI automatically receives and records information on our server logs from your browser or mobile platform, including your location, IP address, and cookie information.  An IP address is a number that is automatically assigned to your computing device when you use the Internet. Cookies are alphanumerical identifiers that are transferred to your computing device’s hard drive through your web browser or mobile platform. Cookies allow our systems to identify the computing device and your browser. IP addresses may be used to help diagnose problems with our server, administer the Website, analyze trends, track users' movement, gather broad demographic information for aggregate use in order for us to improve the Website, and deliver customized, personalized content.  We reserve the right to use cookies and analyze web logs. We may also use third-party services and tools such as Google Analytics, which may provide EI with other information about your use of the Website, but cannot be used to identify you.  These tools help us to understand traffic patterns and ascertain if there are problems with, or opportunities to improve, the Website.  We treat this data as non-Personal Information, except where we are required to do otherwise under applicable law. Unless otherwise stated in this Privacy Policy, EI only uses this data in aggregate form.Collection of Personal InformationIf you create an account on the Website, you will create user credentials such as a user ID and password.  All passwords are encrypted before being stored in our database.  We will only send you your password via an email in response to a specific request for a password reminder associated with the email address that you provide.  If you purchase any products or services through the Website, your purchase will be processed through a secure third party payment processing system.
