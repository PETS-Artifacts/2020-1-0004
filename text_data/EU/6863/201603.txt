We never transfer your data outside   of the UK.COOKIESCookies are small files that a website places on your hard disk to enable additional features of the   website to work. Use of cookies is an industry standard and many websites use them and most internet   browsers automatically accept cookies.The law relating to the use of cookies changed in May 2011.
