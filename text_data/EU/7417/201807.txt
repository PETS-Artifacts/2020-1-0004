
Privacy Policy
You can use this website anonymously. If you are logged out, we will not gather any identifying information about you. If you register an account and log in to it, we will collect your email address.
Your email address will only be used to contact you regarding your account, e.g. to send password reset emails, or to reply to emails you send from the Support pages. We will never pass your email address on to anyone else, unless we are legally required to do so.
If you want to change or delete the email address attached to your account, you can do so using the Edit User Data button in your file list, or by emailing [email protected]
If we ever have a data breach, we will notify affected users by email and by on-site notification within 7 business days of our discovery of the breach.
If we change this Privacy Policy, we will place a notification on all pages with a link to the updated policy, at least 14 days before the changes come into effect.
Cookies
SFS uses cookies to keep you logged in. If you disable cookies, you will have to log in every time you visit the site.
Google Analytics and advertising partners may place cookies on your computers, to anonymously analyse our website traffic, or to tailor adverts to your interests. You can opt out of these cookies by visiting the Network Advertising Initiative Opt Out page or by using the Google Analytics Opt Out Browser add on.
