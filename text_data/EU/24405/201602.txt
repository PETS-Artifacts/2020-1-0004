Our usage of cookies
Cookies are small files stored on your computer by web browsers such as Internet Explorer, Firefox etc. and are used to store information specific to the particular website. These files are not active in any way, are just text based, cannot carry viruses and as such do not pose a risk to your computer. We use cookies to help navigation and ensure only one session is open at a time in relation to our systems. No passwords or other personal information are stored in these.
If you disable cookies then you may not be able to access our website and you will not be able to access your account. For further information on how cookies work and can be managed, a guide can be found at www.allaboutcookies.org.
