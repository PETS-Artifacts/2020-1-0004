Postgraduate Applications Centre - Privacy Statement\r\nPAC is an organisation which was founded in 1998 to process applications \r\ncentrally for admission to taught & research based postgraduate courses in \r\nthird level institutions in the Republic of Ireland. \r\n\r\nThe purpose of the PAC is to process postgraduate applications for higher \r\neducation courses centrally and to deal with them in an efficient and fair manner. \r\n\r\nThe HEIs retain the function of making decisions on admissions.\r\n\r\nThis statement relates to the privacy practices in connection with the\r\nwebsite of the Postgraduate Applications Centre. Links to other websites are \r\nclearly identifiable as such. We are not responsible for the content or \r\nprivacy practices of other websites. \r\n\r\nGeneral statement\r\nThe Postgraduate Applications Centre respects your right to privacy, and will\r\nnot collect any personal information about you on this website without your\r\npermission. Any personal information which you volunteer will be treated \r\nwith the highest standards of security and confidentiality, strictly in \r\naccordance with the Data Protection Acts, 1988 & 2003.\r\n\r\nCollection and use of personal information\r\nThe Postgraduate Applications Centre does not collect any personal data about\r\nyou on this website, apart from information which you volunteer (for example\r\nby e-mailing us or by using our online application services). Any information\r\nwhich you provide in this way is made available only to the participating\r\nHigher Education Institutions and is not made available to any other third\r\nparties. The Information you provide is used only in line with the purpose \r\nfor which you provided it.\r\n\r\n \r\nPlease note that the HEIs to which you apply may wish to\r\ncommunicate with you about your application, and they\r\nmay contact you by email, post or SMS text.\r\n \r\n\r\nYou have a right to be given a copy of any of your personal data which\r\nis retained by the PAC. You may submit a request in writing to the above\r\npostal or email address. For identification purposes you must supply your \r\nfull name as you supplied it to PAC, your date of birth, and your postal\r\naddress (your PAC application number would be of assistance also). PAC\r\nwill provide you with a printed copy of your personal data from our files\r\nwithin 40 days of receiving your written request. PAC reserves the right \r\nto charge a fee not exceeding \x806.35 for this service, however, given the\r\nsmall demand for such services to date, personal data printouts are\r\nusually provided by PAC free of charge.\r\n\r\n \r\nPaper applications and supporting application documents\r\nare retained for two years and then confidentially\r\ndestroyed. Electronic data is retained for a period of three\r\nyears. No documents will be returned to Applicants.\r\n \r\n\r\nCollection and use of technical information\r\nThis website does not use cookies, apart from temporary "session" cookies\r\nwhich enable a visitor\x92s web browser to remember which pages on this website\r\nhave already been visited. Visitors can use this website with no loss\r\nof functionality if cookies are disabled from the web browser.\r\n\r\nTechnical details in connection with visits to this website may be logged\r\nfor statistical purposes. The technical details logged are confined to the\r\nfollowing items:\r\n\r\nthe IP address of the visitor\x92s web server \r\nthe top-level domain name used (for example .ie, .com, .org, .net)\r\nthe previous website address from which the visitor reached us, including any search terms used\r\nclickstream data which shows the traffic of visitors around this web site (for example pages accessed and documents downloaded) \r\nthe type of web browser used by the website visitor.\r\n\r\n\r\nThe Postgraduate Applications Centre will make no attempt to identify individual \r\nvisitors, or to associate the technical details listed above with any individual.\r\nIt is the policy of the Postgraduate Applications Centre never to disclose such \r\ntechnical information in respect of individual website visitors to any third \r\nparty unless obliged to disclose such information by a rule of law. The technical\r\ninformation will be used only by the Postgraduate Applications Centre, and only for \r\nstatistical and other administrative purposes. You should note that technical \r\ndetails, which we cannot associate with any identifiable individual, do not \r\nconstitute "personal data" for the purposes of the Data Protection Acts, 1988 & 2003.\r\n\r\nGlossary of technical terms used\r\n\r\nweb browser\r\n\r\nThe software you use to read web pages. Examples include Microsoft Internet Explorer,\r\nMozilla Firefox, Netscape Navigator, and Opera.\r\n\r\nIP address\r\n\r\nThe identifying details for your computer (or your internet service provider\x92s \r\ncomputer), expressed in "internet protocol" code (for example 192.1.214.10). Every \r\ncomputer connected to the web has a unique IP address, although the address may not\r\nbe the same every time a connection is made.\r\n\r\ncookies\r\n\r\nSmall pieces of information, stored in simple text files, placed on your computer\r\nby a web site. Cookies can be read by the web site on your subsequent visits. The \r\ninformation stored in a cookie may relate to your browsing habits on the web page, \r\nor a unique identification number so that the web site can "remember" you on your \r\nreturn visit. Generally speaking, cookies do not contain personal information from \r\nwhich you can be identified, unless you have furnished such information to the web \r\nsite. \r\n\r\n\r\nPAC is an organisation which was founded in 1998 to process applications \r\ncentrally for admission to taught & research based postgraduate courses in \r\nthird level institutions in the Republic of Ireland. \r\n\r\nThe purpose of the PAC is to process postgraduate applications for higher \r\neducation courses centrally and to deal with them in an efficient and fair manner. \r\n\r\nThe HEIs retain the function of making decisions on admissions.\r\n\r\nThis statement relates to the privacy practices in connection with the\r\nwebsite of the Postgraduate Applications Centre.
