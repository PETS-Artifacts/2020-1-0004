​Cardiff Metropolitan University takes the responsibility towards protecting 
your privacy, when you use our web sites and services, very seriously. This 
statement describes why we may collect information from you and how this will be 
treated. Please note that non Cardiff Metropolitan University web sites, which 
may be linked to, are not covered by this statement. 1. Information CollectionInformation may be collected from you either automatically in an anonymous 
form through the Use of Cookies or alternatively Personal Information may be 
acquired manually by you entering it into web based forms. 2. Use of CookiesWe may store information, about your access to our web sites, using cookies. 
These are files, sent by us to your computer or other access device, which can 
then be accessed when you visit our sites in future. These may be utilised for 
two reasons on Cardiff Metropolitan University web sites and services. a) Cookies are used by Google Analytics, a statistical tool that analyses 
usage of web sites by collecting internet log and behavioural information in an 
anonymous form. This information is then used to report on activity and provide 
an indication of web site usage, enabling us to provide a better service.b) In addition cookies are used to aid with service and system access by 
allowing you to move around within our web sites without having to 
re-authenticate. Such cookies will then normally be automatically removed from 
your computer when you close down your browser windows.For further information on cookies and instructions 
on their control and deletion please visit http://www.aboutcookies.org/. Please 
note that by deleting or disabling future cookies you may not be able to access 
certain areas or features of our web sites or services.3. Personal InformationPersonal Information (such as Name, Address, Telephone Number, etc), that you 
provide to us through the use of web forms, will be stored securely and will 
only be accessed by authorised Cardiff Metropolitan University personnel. We may also disclose your information to third parties, such as service 
providers or agencies, who act for us in providing our services. In such cases 
we will take steps to ensure the information is only used in accordance with the 
terms identified in this document.Unless required or permitted do so by law, we will not otherwise share, sell 
or distribute any of the information you provide to us without your consent.Some URLs are operated for us, Cardiff Metropolitan University by Hobsons plc

University” or “us” equally include any dealings by you with Hobsons where they 
are acting for the Cardiff Met.4. Further InformationIf you have any queries or require further 
clarification on this privacy statement or on the handling of your personal
