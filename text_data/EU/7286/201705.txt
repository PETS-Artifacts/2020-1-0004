Cookies
A cookie is a small piece of data that is sent to your browser from a Web server and stored on your computer’s hard drive. Cookies store information about your use of a particular Web site or service and are typically used to make services more convenient and personalized. Most cookies are “session cookies,” which are automatically deleted whenever you leave a Web site, although some cookies remain on your computer until you remove them. 
Third Party Cookies
In the course of serving advertisements to this site, our third-party advertiser(s) may place or recognize a unique “cookie” on your browser. 
How We Use Cookies
We use cookies (1) to estimate the number of visitors to our Web site, (2) to measure general traffic patterns, and (3) to personalize information and services we provide to you on the site. 
Companies that place ads on the Bloody-Disgusting.com Web site may also use their own cookies to help determine the frequency and placement of particular ads. Your browser controls how cookies are used and can be set to not allow them. However, it is possible that some parts of the Bloody-Disgusting.com site may not function properly if your browser refuses our cookies. 
Cookie Notification and Disabling
Most browsers permit you to set the browser to accept, reject, or notify you of cookie use. However, some parts of the Bloody-Disgusting.com Web site may not function properly if your browser is set to reject cookies.
