Anonymous collection of data on our website
Your browser transmits information which is automatically collected and saved in the server log files of the TeamViewer GmbH. This is e.g.

type/version of the browser
used operating system
referrer URL (the previously visited website)
host name of the accessing computer (IP address)
time of the server request

This data cannot be associated by the TeamViewer GmbH with specific persons. A merging of this data with other data sources is not carried out, the data will be deleted after a statistical analysis.

Use of web analytics tools
This website uses Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google Inc. ("Google"). Google Analytics uses “cookies,” text files that are stored on your computer, to analyze your use of the website. In light of the debate over the use of analytical tools with full IP addresses, please note that this website uses Google Analytics with the extension "_anonymizeIp()"; therefore, IP addresses are used only in abbreviated form to prevent them from being traced back to specific users. Google will use this information on behalf of the operator of this website to analyze your use of the site, create reports on website activity, create reports on impressions in the Google Display Network and provide additional services to the site operator related to website and internet use.
This site uses remarketing with Google Analytics for online advertising, which displays ads on websites by third parties and Google. Cookies are used by this website as well as by third-party providers, including Google, for this purpose. First-party cookies are combined with third-party cookies to tailor and optimize website ads based on previous visits.
You can prevent cookies from being stored by adjusting your browser settings; please note, however, that doing so may prevent you from fully using all of the functions of this website. You may also prevent the information generated by the cookie regarding your use of the website from being sent to or processed by Google by opting out. To do so, you must download and install the browser plug-in available from the following link: http://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout .
You can disable Google Analytics for Display ads and control the kinds of ads shown in the Google Display Network by adjusting your ad settings: http://www.google.com/settings/ads .
Cookies
Our websites use so-called cookies in several places. They help make our offer more user-friendly, effective and secure. Cookies are small text files which are put on your computer and saved by your browser. Most of the cookies used by us are so-called "session cookies" which will be deleted after your visit. Cookies do not cause any damage on your computer and do not contain any viruses.
