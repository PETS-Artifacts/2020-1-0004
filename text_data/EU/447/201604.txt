
Privacy Policy
Information that is gathered from visitors
In common with other websites, log files are stored on the web server saving details such as the visitor’s IP address, browser type, referring page and time of visit. 
The Playground Wiki stores the username for the user modifying a certain page and that is made public to registered users. 
The Forum stores usernames related to messages.
The Single Sign On Arduino server, located at id.arduino.cc stores all the information for a user, username, url, email, etc. That information is private and will not be revealed in any way to others, no matter whether they are registered users or guests in the system from the SSO server. It is possible to configure privacy options locally for different services, like e.g. making one's email address public to other registered users in the Forum.
Cookies may be used to remember visitor preferences when interacting with the website. Cookies are stored on the client side.

How the Information is used
The information is used to enhance the vistor’s experience when using the website to display personalised content.
E-mail addresses as well as any other of the information stored will not be sold, rented or leased to 3rd parties.
E-mail may be sent to inform you of news of our services or offers by us or our affiliates.

Visitor Options
If you have subscribed to one of our services, you may unsubscribe by following the instructions which are provided on our website.
You may be able to block cookies via your browser settings but this may prevent you from access to certain features of the website.

Cookies
Cookies are small digital signature files that are stored by your web browser that allow your preferences to be recorded when visiting the website. 
There are no 3rd party cookies on the Arduino server.

Changes to this policy
This policy may change without prior notice. A new policy will be in effect as soon as it is published on the web site and replace older ones.
