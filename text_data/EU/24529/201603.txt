When you register we ask for information such as your name and email address. Once you register with WealthyAffiliate.com and sign in to our services, you are not anonymous to us.
					WealthyAffiliate.com collects information about your transactions with us.
WealthyAffiliate.com automatically receives and records information on our server logs from your browser, including your IP address, WealthyAffiliate.com cookie information, and the page you request.
WealthyAffiliate.com uses information for the following general purposes: to fulfill your requests for products and services, improve our services, contact you, conduct research, and provide anonymous reporting.
