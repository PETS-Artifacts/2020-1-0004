SoliTaire! Network Privacy Policy:

SoliTaire! Network does not require Login or Registration. We do not attempt to learn your identity in any way unless you offer it to us via email, or a form, or through use of a Social Network.

SoliTaire! Network collects aggregate data such as the percentage of users using a certain browser, how many times games are played in general, how many players are currently playing, and other similar types of information that do not identify you personally.

If you've arrived at SoliTaire Network via an advertisment, we keep track of how many of those people, in aggregate, actually go to a game page. You are not personally identified.

SoliTaire! Network uses Social Network features that you may opt-in to. If you opt-in to these networks, SoliTaire! Network may show limted personal information about you such as your real name and image. You may opt-out again after opting-in at any time. Refer to the privacy policies of the various Social Networks for more information.

SoliTaire! Network uses cookies to store your preferences for such things as which card size you prefer, whether you like to have sounds on or off when you enter a game, results of games won, and other features such as these. A cookie, as used by SoliTaire! Network, is simply a text file that stores simple numbers on your computer. It is not a program that can harm your computer. The SolitaireNetwork.com website does not use cookies as a personal tracking mechanism.

SoliTaire! Network uses third-party vendors, including Google, when you visit this website. These vendors may use cookies to serve ads based on a your prior visits to Solitaire Network. Google's use of the DoubleClick cookie enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to Solitaire Network or other sites on the Internet. You may opt-out of the use of the DoubleClick cookie for interest-based advertising by visiting Ads Settings.
