Our Privacy Policy

How Gamer Network Ltd collects and uses your data

Gamer Network Ltd may ask you for certain information and you may submit personal data to Gamer Network Ltd (such as your name, email address and contact details) when you subscribe or sign up to Rock Paper Shotgun.

Gamer Network Ltd may also record which parts of Rock Paper Shotgun and services you are interested in as well as user traffic patterns and site use. This information will be kept securely in accordance with an internal security policy and may be used to:

  • process your User Content and your subscription to Rock Paper Shotgun and to enable your use of Rock Paper Shotgun and associated services;
  • open and run your account and provide you with an up to date, efficient and reliable service;
  • administer prize draws;
  • generally run Rock Paper Shotgun;
  • send you newsletters and alerts when you sign up for or subscribe to them.

By subscribing and submitting your data you consent to this use. Please see Your Rights to learn how you may change this consent.

Gamer Network Ltd will never collect sensitive information about you without your explicit consent.

If you believe Gamer Network Ltd has information about you that you do not want Gamer Network Ltd to have or that is incorrect, please contact Gamer Network Ltd as described below and Gamer Network Ltd shall correct or remove the data as you request as soon as is reasonably practicable.

Marketing Communications

Gamer Network Ltd gives you a choice whether or not you want Gamer Network Ltd or other reputable companies to contact you about future offers, events and new services or related activities that you may find useful. Gamer Network Ltd won't sell or trade personal information to other companies but Gamer Network Ltd would like to keep you informed of the developments on Rock Paper Shotgun and certain Content and Gamer Network Ltd would like to share data with other reputable companies who may have interesting offers and information for you (inside or outside the EU).

Gamer Network Ltd will not send you any marketing emails or pass your information on to third parties unless you give your consent or you have given it already.

Cookies

Gamer Network Ltd uses 'cookies' on Rock Paper Shotgun. Cookies are a way that information such as whether you have visited Rock Paper Shotgun before is recorded on your computer and used by Gamer Network Ltd to improve Rock Paper Shotgun and the services available to you.

If you do not wish for Gamer Network Ltd to use cookies in any way when you use Rock Paper Shotgun please adjust your Internet browser settings to not accept cookies. Your computer's help file should tell you how to do this but please note that this may affect your ability to use Rock Paper Shotgun and the Content.

If you don't disable "cookies" Rock Paper Shotgun will ask for and store details including name and email address and may automatically insert certain information on the Content submission forms so that you can use Rock Paper Shotgun with less form filling.
