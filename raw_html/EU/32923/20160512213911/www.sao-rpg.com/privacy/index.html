    • More rule adjustments   04/07/2016

      After some discussion and thought, i am implementing some balancing changes. 1. Have the damage return on Vengeful Riposte reduced to a 1/4. Possibility of high damage (despite mitigateable) along with the added 1/2 reduction received could be a bit much. 2. Increase max damage on rapier strongest SA to 14x. Also the Rank 5 SAs on Wild Dance have been buffed a smidge. The SA's will receive a bigger overview at a later date. 3. Increase activation rate of Recovery enhancement to CD 6 or higher to align with Regen. 4. Increasing power of Electrolytes to regenerating 3 energy per slot used. 5. The consumption of consumables also requires the level per tier like equipment. Crystals are the only item (aside from obvious such as banners or otherwise explicitly mentioned) which can be used on others by post effect, where the person receiving the buff does not need to meet the requirements. All other types of consumables will need to be traded to them to equip. The energy recovery skill shown in support was considered, but in the end I don't want to trivialise the energy system and I feel it would make inherently the energy hungry weapons more favourable. More rounded weapons such as OHSS and spear would become less attractive. To take the time to eat food and recover I feel is a good thing as repercussion for burning energy quickly instead of pacing out attacks.

    • Floor benchmarks have been abolished.   04/22/2016

      There are no longer any floor benchmarks. This has been done to reflect the fact that there are no magical barriers in SAO preventing you from rushing to your death. However there is some friendly advice on how you should approach floor bosses.
       

Privacy Policy

The team here at Sword Art Online Roleplay Community respect your privacy and will not share any of your registration information at any time with any users, partners, affiliates, third-party contents without your expressed written permission. In addition, all information stored in our database, while protected by security systems and our privacy terms, is viewable only to the highest level of staff member on this site; this includes your posts, internet service provider, email address, encrypted password, private messages, and file uploads. Therefore if you wish not for any of the mentioned related information to be saved on our database, please do not share or provide any said information without thorough consideration on your part.
