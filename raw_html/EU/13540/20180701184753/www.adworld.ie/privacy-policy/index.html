The use of Cookies

In common with other websites, Adworld.ie and the publisher, Adworld Ltd, will use cookies to collect information about the users of the websites the company publishes including Adworld.ie, OnlineMediaGuide.ie, MediaEye.ie.

Cookies are small pieces of information sent by a web server to a web browser which allows that server to uniquely identify the browser on each page. There are different types of cookies which we use which we have outlined below. This list may change in the future. The data stored by these cookies never shows personal details about the user and the individual identity of users cannot be established.

Advertising Cookies: These cookies collect information about your browsing habits in order to make advertising more relevant to you. These cookies are also used to limit the number of times a viewer sees an advert. In addition, advertising cookies also help advertisers measure the effectiveness of an advertising campaign. Typically, these cookies are placed by a third party entity like an advertising network, a media agency or an advertising client. These cookies remember the websites you visit and the information is shared with other parties like advertisers. The publisher does not have access to these third party advertising cookies.

Social Media Cookies: This site allows the sharing of information and content through social media networks and channels including the likes of Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn and Google + . These social media channels are not within the control of the publisher and you should refer to the particular social media platform where they have outlined their own terms and conditions relating to the use of cookies.

If you want to delete any cookies that are already on your computer, you are free to do so. To disable cookies, please go to the Settings in the support area of your particular browser. Please note that by deleting cookies or disabling future cookies you may not be able to access certain areas or features of our site and/or certain areas within the social media platforms which are used to support this site.
