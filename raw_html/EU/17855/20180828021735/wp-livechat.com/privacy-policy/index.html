Code Cabin and WPLC relies on your consent for data processing purposes. The GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) defines consent as “…freely given, specific, informed…, a statement or by a clear affirmative action, signifies agreement to the processing of personal data…”.

Data submitted on our Website (https://wp-livechat.com/)

 

Data Submitted via Live Chat

As provider of a Live Chat service, WPLC hosts a live chat box on its site (https://wp-livechat.com/). If you, as website visitor, choose to get in touch with our agents via this Live Chat box, you must first consent to your data being processed for support/chat related purposes. Consent is verified by means of a check box and a notice (live chat consent clause) stipulating the data retention period. Based on this consent, WPLC may receive your name and email and information relating to your browser, operating system, type (new or returning visitor), agent ratings, visiting page url and any other personally identifiable information you may provide during the chat process. The latter information is utilized for enhanced support provision and specifically excludes location/IP tracking, so as to further ensure the protection of your personal data privacy. Once this chat data has been processed, it is stored on WPLC’s site database for the period specified in the live chat consent clause but may be requested, rectified or erased on your request, at any time.

 

Data Submitted via Form Entry

Any data submitted to Code Cabin and WPLC by means of our contact form, is reserved for specific product support and query related purposes. If you choose to get in touch with our agents via this form, you must first consent to your data being processed. Consent is verified by means of a check box and a notice stipulating the details of the data controller (Code Cabin and WPLC) and the data retention period. Based on this consent,  Code Cabin and WPLC may receive your name, email address and website url and information relating to the support query. This information is processed and stored on our internal support desk (not a third-party service, thereby providing an additional safety measure) and is retained for as long as reasonably required to act as reference for returning queries. You will be prompted to consent to the retention of you data for a reasonably required period. Only acting support agents of Code Cabin and WPLC will have access to said information, subject to the signing of a confidentiality agreement. Your information may be requested, rectified or erased upon request, at any time.

Cookies

Our Products make use of ‘cookies’. ‘Cookies’ are information bits sent by the server and stored on the relevant device/desktop. Before starting a live chat with us, site visitors are prompted to acknowledge and accept the use of cookies for the cuntining of the chat box. User may revoke such consent at any time.

 

Cookie information employed by WPLC:

  • wplc_name (the name entered in the name input field)
  • wplc_email (the email entered in the email input field)
  • wplc_cid (Cookie that stores the chat ID)
  • wplc_hide (Cookie that hides the chat)
  • wplc_minimize (Cookie that minimizes the chat)
  • wplc_chat_status (Cookie that keeps track of current status of the chat, used to determine if the chat has been initiated or if the user is simply browsing the site)

Users who remove the use of ‘cookies’ will not be able to access certain services or products rendered by Code Cabin and WPLC. Code Cabin and brand representatives have no control over third party cookies and is therefore not covered by this Policy.

By consenting to the use of Our Product, the User consents to the use of cookies, web beacons and log file information to operate Our Product.
