1. INFORMATION COLLECTION PRACTICES
 
(a) Traffic Data.
 
Like most website operators, WenXueCity automatically gathers information of the sort that browsers automatically make available, including: (i) IP addresses; (ii) domain servers; (iii) types of computers accessing the Website; and (iv) types of Web browsers accessing the Website (collectively “Traffic Data”). Traffic Data is anonymous information that does not personally identify You.
 
Additionally, WenXueCity and Google, Inc. collect non-anonymous traffic information about your online activity related to your visit to the Website, including Your use of your devices, Your use of other mobile applications on the same mobile device, Your click data, and Your click patterns in relation to promotional advertising on your device, cookies (defined below), tracking tags, pixel tags, and visitor history.  
 
(b) Cookie Policy
 
“Cookies” are text files that are placed on your computer by websites that you visit or certain emails that you open.  A Cookie acts as a string of information that a website stores on a user’s computer, and that the user’s browser provides to the website each time the user submits a query to the website.  The purpose of a Cookie is to identify the user as a unique user of the Website. WenXueCity uses Cookies.
 
WenXueCity uses cookies on the Website for the following purposes:
  • to customize your experience on the Website to your interests
  • to ensure that you do not see the same advertisements or informational messages repeatedly, and
  • to store your password so you do not have to re-enter it each time you visit the Website. 
IF YOU DO NOT WISH TO HAVE COOKIES PLACED ON YOUR COMPUTER, YOU SHOULD SET YOUR BROWSERS TO REFUSE COOKIES BEFORE ACCESSING THE WEBSITE, WITH THE UNDERSTANDING THAT CERTAIN OF THE SERVICES AND CERTAIN FEATURES OF THE WEBSITE MAY NOT FUNCTION PROPERLY WITHOUT THE AID OF COOKIES.  IF YOU REFUSE COOKIES, YOU ASSUME ALL RESPONSIBILITY FOR ANY RESULTING LOSS OF FUNCTIONALITY.
 
You can learn more about how to delete or control cookies at www.aboutcookies.org. 
 
(c) Ad Serving. 
 
WenXueCity designs its own ad serving system as well as uses third-parties for advertising and analytics for pages that we host. They may place cookies on Your machine, use web beacons, gather IP addresses and log data to collect traffic and activity data to send to the United States in order to deliver relevant metrics, content, and advertising. 
 
(d) Personal Information.
 
In order for You to use certain of the Services, You will be asked to provide WenXueCity certain information that personally identifies You (“Personal Information”).  Personal Information includes, without limitation: (1) “Contact Data” (such as Your contact name, mailing address, phone number, and e-mail address) and (2) “Demographic Data” (such as Your gender and zip code). You are under no obligation to provide Personal Information, with the caveat that Your refusal to do so may prevent you from using certain of the Services.

The Website may contain features (e.g. posting, blogging, instant messages, electronic messaging, etc.) that permit You to upload, post, transmit, display, perform or distribute content, information or other, including Your Personal Information.  Any information that You choose to disclose by means of such features becomes public information over which WenXueCity is unable to exercise control.  You should exercise caution when deciding to disclose Your Personal Information by means of such features, and You agree to assume all responsibility for doing so.

The WenXueCity Mobile Application may collect: (i) information about the type, make, and model of Your device, the operating system You use on the device, browser type and specifications, the network service or wireless communication provider who connects Your device to a telecommunications system or systems, Your internet protocol address, and other unique identifiers assigned by third parties to Your mobile device, and (ii) information regarding the physical geographic location of your device.
