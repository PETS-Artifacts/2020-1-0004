SYNC.ME PRIVACY POLICY

Last Revised : May 21 th , 2018

INTRODUCTION

Sync.Me (“we”, "us" or the "Company") is firmly committed to ensure that the personal information that users (“user” or “you”) share with us and with other users in our community in connection with the use of our mobile application and/or by accessing our website (collectively the "Service") is safeguarded and protected.

Our Service is primarily designed to empower consumers to make informed decisions and deter illegal and fraudulent calling activities. We are dedicated to ensure that our activity is firmly grounded upon a legitimate interest, and that the privacy expectations of consumers are carefully weighed and reasonably balanced against such legitimate purposes.

Our Service plays a key role in enhancing connectivity and thwarting intrusive and fraudulent calls, by introducing a variety of smart features that empower consumers to make informed decisions and reducing the risks of the user community from being targeted by malicious or misleading activities, including:

  • Reporting and blocking spam : our users can choose to report and block numbers to be added to their personal spam call list, and potentially a regional spam call list. In the event a certain number was reported as spam by a certain number of users, other users will be notified if they receive a call from that same number.
  • Live Caller ID and Spam detection : our Live Caller ID displays the name of the caller before the consumer answers the call, and flags if the number was reported as spam.
  • Number search (reverse lookup) : individuals, including those who are not registered users with Sync.ME, may manually search a number on Sync.ME's web-based platform in order to ascertain the identity of the caller and whether a number is spam.

The contact information collected by Sync.ME is used solely to empower individuals to identify incoming calls from unknown sources, identify unknown numbers through the use of reverse numeric lookup, as well as to report and block fraudulent, intrusive or inappropriate calls. We designed our features to better respond to deceptive and abusive practices aided by anonymity, both in telemarketing as well as in other abusive or deceptive practices (such as abusive calls).

Our goal is to promote consumers' privacy by allowing them to know who is calling them, as well as promoting accountability throughout the industry .
