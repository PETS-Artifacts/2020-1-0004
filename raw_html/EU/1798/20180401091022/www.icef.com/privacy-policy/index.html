About ICEF

ICEF connects Educators, Education Agents, Work & Travel Professionals, and Industry Service Providers to key markets and networks worldwide, supporting the growth and development of international education and global student mobility. Through ICEF’s various services, education institutions and agents achieve results and efficiencies in growing international student enrolments.
