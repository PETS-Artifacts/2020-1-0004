Your privacy

Privacy notices and links

At Loughborough University we believe strongly in protecting our users' privacy. We will not wilfully disclose information about our users to any third party without first receiving our users' consent. Loughborough University takes the privacy and data protection of the individuals it interacts with seriously. This page tells you more about our approach to your data and provides tools to help you manage your relationship with us.

Our privacy notices set out in more detail how we use personal data and what your rights are in respect of that data. You have right to request a copy of the personal information we hold about you, via the Subject Access Rights process and you can do that from this page. We hope you find everything you need on these pages, but if not please contact us by emailing our Data Protection Officer. You can also write to us addressed to: Data Protection Officer, Academic Registry, Loughborough University, Loughborough, Leics, UK LE11 3TU.
