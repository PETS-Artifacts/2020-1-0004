The Privacy Policy:

We give a lot of importance to the protection of your data. We have established safety and confidential rules to ensure the best possible protection of your data. We do, however, need to collect certain information when you order. Please know that personal data is not collected without your prior consent.

These rules apply to all those involved with the company.

What data is collected?

During registration, ordering or when contacting the Instant Gaming Ltd company, personal information such as your name, email address and telephone number can be requested. It is your wish as to whether or not you provide this information. Should you choose not to, Instant Gaming Ltd will not be in a position to provide you with products, services or the requested information. No bank details are ever recorded by Instant Gaming Ltd.

We never record your bank data.

Technical data

When you access our website, technical data is stored in the server's log files, i.e. your I.P. address, the URL, the date and time, as well as your browser version and operating system. In addition, cookies are sometimes sent to your hard drive. Cookies are small text files stored temporarily on your hard drive and may contain information about your visit to our website. They facilitate navigation and allow you to customise the display of the site for further ease of use.
The data collected is of a technical nature and never recorded; only anonymous statistics are collected.

We don't use cookies to track you, but to avoid asking for your password every 15 minutes

Navigational statistics

To improve the usability of our website, we collect navigational data for statistical purposes. We use it for the services of Google Analytics. From this data, usage patterns are created and pseudonyms are assigned to them. Data is collected and recorded under a pseudonym only. The data is not used to personally identify the visitor to this website or to establish a connection with the personal information of the holder of the pseudonym. This ensures that no connection to the data protocol mentioned above or those of the user profile can be established.

We collect stats to understand better our users, but don't worry, they're anonymous

Removal of collected data

Your personal data collected is eliminated when you withdraw your consent for collection, when it is no longer required for initial collection purposes or when its collection is prohibited for legal reasons.

We will erase your data just with a simple request...

Stored data access

You have the right to access any of your personal data collected by Instant Gaming Ltd and at no cost. You also have a right to correct, update or delete data. You can revoke your consent at any time on the collection, processing and use of personal data concerning you.
Security: we take all appropriate technical and organisational measures to safeguard the security of your personal data and to prevent third party access. We cannot guarantee complete security of data when communicating by email. If you wish to communicate confidential information, we recommend you to use a postal service.
