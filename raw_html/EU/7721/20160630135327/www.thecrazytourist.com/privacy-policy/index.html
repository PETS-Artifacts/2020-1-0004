Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

The privacy of our visitors is of extreme importance to us. This privacy policy document outlines the types of personal information received and collected by our website and how it is used and protected.

Log Files

Like many other Web sites we makes use of log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol ( IP ) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider ( ISP ), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track users movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable. Data collected by us will not be shared with others.

Cookies and Web Beacons

We use cookies to store information about visitors preferences, record user-specific information on which pages the user accesses or visits, customize Web page content based on visitors browser type or other information that the visitor sends via their browser. For more information about cookies please visit: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/HTTP_cookie

Advertisers

Some of our advertising partners may use cookies and web beacons on our site. Our advertising partners include: Google Adsense, Sovrn, Media.net and Criteo. If you’d like to learn more about how advertising companies show ads based on your online behavior I suggest reading this article from truste.com.

DoubleClick DART Cookie

Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on The Crazy Tourist. Google’s use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to our users based on their visit to thecrazytourist.com and other sites on the Internet. You may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy at the following URL:  http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html.

If you would have any more questions you can contact us directly.
