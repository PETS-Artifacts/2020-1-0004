Privacy Policy – Drivereasy.com

This Policy was last updated on October 30, 2017

 

Welcome to drivereasy.com (“drivereasy.com”, “Driver Easy”, “we”, “us”, or “our”). Driver Easy is a driver tool developed by us. Supported by huge hardware driver database, Driver Easy gives customers fast access to the latest driver versions using a driver scanner.

We have developed this privacy policy to demonstrate its commitment to protecting your privacy. These privacy policies (the “Privacy Policy”) describes our data privacy practices, including what type of information is gathered, how we uses the information, and how we safeguard your Personal Information when you submit it to us at www.drivereasy.com.

Your privacy is important to us. To better protect your privacy we provide this notice explaining our information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used. To make this notice easy to find, we make it available on our website.

 

Why Driver Easy Collects Personal Information?

Your device’s hardware and software inventory information would be collected by Driver Easy in order to deliver our driver and other device-associated recommendations, download access, and installation automations provided in Driver Easy. The hardware and software inventory data is crucial for Driver Easy and servers to match the best updates driver for your device. We obtain all this information in order to improve our service for you.

Personal Information We Collect

Driver Easy collects your Personal Information when you place an order, (which may require you to provide personal information, including a credit card number, to our commerce providers) register software, request services or take part in other activities on our Web site. During these interactions, Driver Easy collects relative Personal Information, such as your name, e-mail address, credit card information along with information about the Driver Easy, such as the activation code, date of purchase and information. We use this information for billing purposes and to fill your orders. If we have trouble processing an order, we will use this information to contact you. We will not use your personal information for bad purpose. Please be aware that if you prefer not to provide us with your Personal Information, because such information may be necessary for us to provide our services, we may not be able to make such services available to you.

 

Information for Device Hardware and Software

The scanning technology present in Driver Easy is designed to collect all hardware ID’s and software signatures present in the system and all this data would be sent back to our servers. We use this information so that our servers can respond back to our software with a list of recommended drivers. This information is also used to support any technical, product, or customer support request. Please be aware that if you would not like to provide this inventory information, our software may not be able to provide you with services.

 

Cookies and Other Technologies

Cookies

To make drivereasy.com work properly, we sometimes place small data files called cookies on your device.

A cookie is a small text file that a website saves on your computer or mobile device when you visit the site. It enables the website to remember your actions and preferences over a period of time, so you don’t have to adjust preferences whenever you come back to the site or browse from one page to another.

Drivereasy.com will use cookies to remember your actions and preferences in order to provide you with better service when you make return visits. Every cookie drivereasy.com assigns to your device which access to our servers is non-unique and non-identifiable. All drivereasy.com cookies are characterized as secure and non-invasive. The information that we collect is not used to identify you personally and the pattern data is fully under our control.

Drivereasy.com will ask whether you accept cookies when you access our website, and we won’t place cookies on your device without your consent. You can also delete all cookies that are already on your device and set most browsers to prevent them from being placed. For details to delete or control cookies on your device, please consult the documentation of your web browser. If you disable cookies, however, you may have to manually adjust some preferences every time you visit drivereasy.com and some services and functionalities may not work.

Google Analytics

We use a tool called “Google Analytics” to collect information about visitor behavior on drivereasy.com. Google Analytics collects information about what pages the visitors visit, how long the visitors are on the website, how the visitors get to the website and what the visitors click. Google Analytics collects only visitors’ IP address, rather than their personal information that can identify who they are (like their names and addresses). We use the information to improve our website according to visitor behavior so that we can provide the visitors with better experience, and we do not use it for other purposes.

Google Analytics places a cookie on your web browser to identify you as a unique visitor the next time you visit our website, but the cookie can only be used by Google. You can disable cookies on your browser to prevent Google Analytics from identifying you when you come back to drivereasy.com. You can also install Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on on your web browser to prevent your data from being used by Google Analytics.

 

Security

Driver Easy web site has security measures in order to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. All the data regarding customers, users, their computers’ system information and data are stored on a server. This server is located in a locked and guarded space and only authorized personnel have access to the database containing the stored information.

 

Other websites’ link

Our Site includes links to other Web sites, whose privacy practices may differ from those in drivereasy.com. If you access other sites using the links provided, the operators of these sites may collect information from you, your information is governed by their privacy statements. We do suggest you to carefully read the privacy statement of any Web site you visit.

Acceptance for Privacy Policy

By using the Web site or services provided on drivereasy.com, you would agree to the use and disclosure of information stated in this Privacy Policy.

 

Changes Notification

We may modify or update this Privacy Policy from time to time, so please review it periodically. We will post any changes, along with effective date, in this page. Your continued use of Driver Easy or the Service after any modification to this Privacy Policy will constitute your acceptance of such modification.

 

Contacting the Web Site

If you have any questions or comments about this privacy statement, the practices of our sites, your dealings with any of our websites, or driver problem when using our software, please feel free to contact support@drivereasy.com
