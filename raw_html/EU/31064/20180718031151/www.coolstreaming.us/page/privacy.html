What are cookies?

A cookie is a small text file that sites save on your computer or mobile device while you visit them. Thanks to cookies, the site remembers your actions and preferences (eg login, language, font size, and other display settings) so you do not have to re-enter it when you return to the site or navigate from one page to another.
