You can "Opt-out" from having a cookie identifier stored on your device when ads are shown from Adform’s advertising platform. To "Opt-out" click the button at your right once. You will then have your cookie data wiped, leaving you only with a new cookie identifier which indicates to our system not to track your browser when displaying ads in this browser. Please be aware that cookies are different on other browsers and devices so this action only applies to this browser and device.
