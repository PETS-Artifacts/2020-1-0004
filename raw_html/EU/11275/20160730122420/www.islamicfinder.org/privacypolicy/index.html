What is this Privacy Policy for?

This privacy policy is for IslamicFinder's website [www.islamicfinder.org] and served by IslamicFinder and maintains the privacy of its users.

The policy addresses the various areas where user privacy is concerned and defines the obligations and requirements of the website users, the website itself and the website owners. Moreover, the way this website protects, stores and processes user data and information will also be given in detail in this policy.

The Website

The IslamicFinder's website and it's owners take a proactive approach regarding user privacy and make sure that the necessary steps are taken to protect the privacy of its users throughout their website browsing experience.

Use of Cookies

The IslamicFinder website uses cookies to improve the users experience while browsing/visiting the website. This website uses a cookie control system which enables the user to allow or disallow the use of cookies on their computer / device upon their first website visit. This complies with recent legislation requirements for websites to attain explicit consent from their users before leaving behind or reading files such as cookies on a user's computer / device.

Cookies are small files that are saved to the user's computer's hard drive which track, save and store information about the user's interactions and usage of the website. This allows the website to provide its users with a personalized experience.

In order for our users to disallow or discontinue the use and saving of cookies from this website on their computer's hard drive, necessary steps will have to be taken within their web browsers security settings which will block all website cookies and it's external serving vendors.

This website uses tracking software to monitor it's visitors to better understand their website usage. This tracking software is provided by Google Analytics, which uses cookies to monitor visitor's usage. The software will automatically save a cookie to your computer's hard drive in order to track your engagement and usage of the IslamicFinder website, but it won't collect, save or store personal information of users. You can read Google's privacy policy for further information [http://www.google.com/privacy.html].

Other cookies might be saved to your computer's hard drive by external websites or vendors when this website uses referral programs, sponsored links or advertisements. Such cookies are used for conversion and referral tracking, which typically expire within 30 days, though some may take longer. No personal information is saved, collected or stored.

Users who contact IslamicFinder’s website and/or its owners do so at their own discretion and provide personal details that are requested at their own risk. We have ensured a safe and secure form for an email submission process but we advise our users using such form to email processes that they do so at their own risk.

This website and its owners may use information submitted by you in order to provide further information about the products / services we offer or to assist you in answering any questions or queries submitted. This includes using your details to get you subscribed to any email newsletter program the website handles but it is only done with your permission. Or if you have previously purchased from or enquired about purchasing from the company about a certain product or service that the email newsletter relates to. Your personal details are never passed on to any third parties.

Users can subscribe through an online automated process if they want to do so but do so at their own discretion. Some of the subscriptions may be manually processed through a previously written agreement with the user.
