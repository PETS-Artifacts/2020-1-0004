Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2018

Our commitment to privacy

You deserve to know how we collect and use the data you provide to us, we are providing that information on this page. The notice applies to all information collected or submitted on the AgriLand website or our sister sites, specifically Agriretailer, Agrirecruit and our Advertising microsite. This notice was updated in April 2018 to reflect GDPR compliance standards. If you are not happy with our privacy policy, then you should not continue using this site.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use our services:

  • Information supplied when you use the website or apps
  • Information which you otherwise provide to us (for example by email or private message); and
  • Information relating to your use of Agriland.ie generally. This may include, for example the polls you’ve voted on or stories you’ve read

Technical details in connection with visits to this website are logged for our statistical purposes. The technical details logged include:

  • The IP address of each visitor and the domain of the internet service provider;
  • The previous website address from which a visitor reached us, including any search terms used;
  • The type of web browser used by a visitor.
  • Mobile device identifier or other unique identifier
  • Computer type
  • Adverts that you click on or adverts that were displayed to you

We and our third-party service providers (including any third-party content, advertising, and analytics providers) may automatically collect certain information from your device or web browser when you interact with the Services. This will be referred to as “Usage Data” from hereon. Our primary third-party service provider is Google Analytics. Further information on what they collect can be found here.

Information You Choose To Submit

Additionally we may collect and store personal information which you voluntarily provide to us. You provide this information to us when you sign up for a newsletter, competition or to access other content.

The type of information collected on those pages generally includes but is not limited to:

  • Email address
  • Name
  • Company
  • Farming Enterprise
  • Location/Address
  • Farm Information

We will never share this information with outside parties unless you explicitly agree to it.

How We Use This Information

We and our third-party service providers may use such Usage Data for a variety of purposes including to diagnose problems with our servers and software, to administer the Services, to gather demographic information and to target advertising to you on the Services and elsewhere online.

We will also use this information to better provide you with content and information we think you will be interested in.

Accordingly, our third-party advertising networks and ad servers will also provide us with information, including reports that will tell us how many ads were presented and clicked on the Services in a manner that does not identify personally any specific individual.

The Usage Data we collect is generally non-identifying, but if we associate it with you as a specific and identifiable person, we will treat it as Personal Data.

Unless explicitly stated we do not share, sell or rent this information with any outside parties.

Cookies are created not just by Agriland.ie but also by other websites that run ads, or other elements on the Agriland.ie website.  We use third party advertisements to support our site. Some of these advertisers may use technology such as cookies and web beacons when they advertise on our site, which will also send these advertisers information including your IP address, your ISP, the browser you used to visit our site, and in some cases, whether you have Flash installed. This is generally used for geo-targeting purposes or showing certain ads based on specific sites visited.

Controlling Cookies

You are free to disable cookies by modifying your browser preferences. However it is important to note that you may lose functions of this site if you choose to restrict or disable cookies. Cookies are meant to improve the user experience.
