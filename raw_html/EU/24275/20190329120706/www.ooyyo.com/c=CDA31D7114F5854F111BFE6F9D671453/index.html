About us

Welcome to OOYYO, the place where you can find millions of used cars!

OOYYO is a leading global vertical price comparison engine for used cars. The service aggregates and semantically analyzes millions of cars for sale from thousands of different information sources, returning the most relevant search results to the users. At the moment, OOYYO is present in:

  • 34 car markets and available in
  • 31 different languages.

OOYYO has the largest index in the world, with more than 10 million unique cars on sale, from more than 25 million different listings from online sources worldwide.

What we do

Since our price comparison engine gathers and organizes millions of listings from all over the world into one place, there is no longer need to browse dozens of car websites to find the car you’ve been searching for. That makes OOYYO the best way to buy and sell used cars through online classifieds - ever. The new, improved design offers numerous features that will make your search even more efficient:

  • Possibility to search for cars in 34 countries worldwide and in your own language;
  • Car reviews written by our car experts;
  • Many various options for narrowing down your search easily by using our numerous filters;
  • Comparing up to five different cars;
  • Marking your favorite car ads;
  • Saved history of all your car searches;
  • Sorting listings by your favorite criteria;
  • Sorting filters by your favorite criteria;
  • Possibility to read comments of other buyers or car enthusiasts and to write your own remarks.

Want to see your ads on OOYYO?

Many car classified websites have already chosen to promote their car offer through our price comparison engine, as we deliver free traffic and high quality visitors to those websites, by redirecting our users to the originating website. If you own a classified ads website, and want to include car ads from your website in OOYYO’s listings, please contact us. If you are a private seller and wish to see your car listed on OOYYO, just post your ad with any of the websites present in our comparison shopping engine, and it will be automatically added to our listings with the next database update.

Want to get in touch with us?

We constantly strive to improve our price comparison website for used cars in order to make your car search even more efficient, easy and fun. Should you have any suggestions, questions or concerns, please feel free to contact us.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small files that a site or its service provider transfers to your computer's hard drive through your Web browser that enables the sites or service provider’s systems to recognize your browser and capture and remember certain information.

In addition to cookies, we use browser local storage technology to store your preferences and make your user experience better. Local storage is a persistent type of storage, that provides similar functionality to cookies, but its contents are not transmitted across the Internet, like cookies are. Same as with cookies, you can control how local storage works on your browser settings on a global or website specific manner.

How do we use cookies?

When you use and access our Website, we may place a number of cookies files in your web browser. We use cookies to compile aggregated data about site traffic and site interaction so that we can offer better site experiences and tools in the future.

Frequently asked questions

1. I have a car for sale and want to advertise it on your site. How do I do that?

OOYYO is price comparison engine for used cars which displays cars for sale from other websites through classified ads. Therefore, it is not possible for an individual to advertise directly on OOYYO. However, if you want your car to be shown on our site, you can advertise it on websites whose content is being displayed on OOYYO and your ad will then appear in our listings.

2. I own a website with classified car ads and want to include them on your site.

You can add your cars by sending us an XML feed with the listing of the cars from your website. All the information you need for setting up an XML feed can be found here: submission.

3. I found a car I’m interested in, but I don’t know how to contact the seller.

On the page with the detailed description of your chosen car, you’ll find the link Contact seller. By clicking on that link, you will be redirected to the ad on the originating website. On that page you can find the seller’s contact information.

4. I want to buy a car found on your website.

All information about the conditions for purchase can be found on the originating website, by clicking on the link Contact seller on the page with the detailed description of your chosen car. OOYYO does not own the cars in the listings and therefore takes no part in the sale.

5. I want to buy a car found on your website and import it to another country. Where can I find information about exporting?

OOYYO is not responsible for the sale, purchase, export or import of cars from its listings. For more information on purchase and export conditions, please contact the seller whose information can be found by clicking on the Contact seller link on the page with the detailed description of the chosen car.

6. I found a car I want to buy, but the ad has been removed from/has expired on the originating website.

Despite the fact that data on OOYYO is updated on a daily basis, the selection of cars changes from hour to hour. The car you found has been sold (or removed for some other reason) subsequent to OOYYO collecting the information on the car in question.

7. Why is the price of a car in some cases different on OOYYO from the price on the originating website?

The car dealer or owner has changed the price after OOYYO collected the information from that website.

8. I want to buy a car abroad but I don’t speak the language of that country.

OOYYO allows you to search for cars in 34 countries in the language of your choice from a range of 31 languages. First, choose the desired language from the drop-down menu in the upper right corner and then the search country by selecting the desired country in the search box on the homepage or the results page.

9. I have a suggestion, idea or proposal for cooperation.

If you haven’t found the answer to your question here, or you have a proposal for cooperation with OOYYO, please contact us by filling out the form on the Contact us page.

BENEFITS OF AN XML FEED! Why an XML feed?

Improve the visibility of your ads on OOYYO

We would like to explain the way in which we generate data from your site, and the benefits of XML feeds.

Our crawling program indexes your site page by page, beginning with the home page, or using your sitemap.xml if you have one, respecting the rules in your robots.txt file in doing so.

A more accurate and practical way of doing this is by using XML feeds. By sending us data on all your vehicles via XML feeds means:

  • 100% fresh data with no expired links
  • 100% accurate data without malformed URLs and similar problems that are possible when a site is indexed manually.

Since OOYYO's algorithm favours the freshest results with the cleanest data, if you use XML feeds, vehicles from your site will appear more often than other results.

Benefits of an XML feed and  How to setup an XML feed

How to setup an XML feed

Please keep in mind that we only accept feeds containing vehicle listings. We do not include bikes, motorcycles, scooters, boats, car parts, etc.

Upon receiving your feed, we will index your ads on OOYYO as soon as possible.

If you have any questions or concerns, please don’t hesitate to contact us!

XML Feed Format

Here are some simple guidelines for preparing an XML feed so that wecan include your ads on OOYYO. If you would like additionalinformation about the XML format, check out Wikipedia.

The UTF-8 XML file has one ‘ooyyo’ tag, and one or more ‘ad’ elements. Each ‘ad’ element has the following fields:

  • url: URL of the ad on your website
  • title: title of the ad
  • content: description of the car
  • price: price of the car (integer value) / set the "unit" to currency of your country
  • example:

    price

  • model: model of the car
  • engine_size: engine size of the car / set the "unit" to cc (cubic centimeter) or l (litre) (optional)
  • example:
