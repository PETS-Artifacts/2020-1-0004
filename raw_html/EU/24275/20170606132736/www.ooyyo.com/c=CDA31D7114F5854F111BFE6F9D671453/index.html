About us

Welcome to OOYYO, the place where you can find millions of used cars!

OOYYO is a leading global vertical search engine for used cars. The service aggregates and semantically analyzes millions of cars for sale from thousands of different information sources, returning the most relevant search results to the users. At the moment, OOYYO is present in:

  • 26 car markets and available in
  • 26 different languages.

OOYYO has the largest index in the world, with more than 15 million unique cars on sale, from more than 25 million different listings from online sources worldwide.

What we do

Since our search engine gathers and organizes millions of listings from all over the world into one place, there is no longer need to browse dozens of car websites to find the car you’ve been searching for. That makes OOYYO the best way to buy and sell used cars through online classifieds - ever. The new, improved design offers numerous features that will make your search even more efficient:

  • Possibility to search for cars in 46 countries worldwide and in your own language;
  • Car reviews written by our car experts;
  • Many various options for narrowing down your search easily by using our numerous filters;
  • Comparing up to five different cars;
  • Marking your favorite car ads;
  • Saved history of all your car searches;
  • Sorting listings by your favorite criteria;
  • Sorting filters by your favorite criteria;
  • Possibility to read comments of other buyers or car enthusiasts and to write your own remarks.

Want to see your ads on OOYYO?

Many car classified websites have already chosen to promote their car offer through our search engine, as we deliver free traffic and high quality visitors to those websites, by redirecting our users to the originating website. If you own a classified ads website, and want to include car ads from your website in OOYYO’s listings, please contact us. If you are a private seller and wish to see your car listed on OOYYO, just post your ad with any of the websites present in our search engine, and it will be automatically added to our listings with the next database update.

Want to get in touch with us?

We constantly strive to improve our search engine in order to make your car search even more efficient, easy and fun. Should you have any suggestions, questions or concerns, please feel free to contact us.

Frequently asked questions

1. I have a car for sale and want to advertise it on your site. How do I do that?

OOYYO is used car search engine which displays cars for sale from other websites through classified ads. Therefore, it is not possible for an individual to advertise directly on OOYYO. However, if you want your car to be shown on our site, you can advertise it on websites whose content is being displayed on OOYYO and your ad will then appear in our listings.

2. I own a website with classified car ads and want to include them on your site.

You can add your cars by sending us an XML feed with the listing of the cars from your website. All the information you need for setting up an XML feed can be found here: submission.

3. I found a car I’m interested in, but I don’t know how to contact the seller.

On the page with the detailed description of your chosen car, you’ll find the link Contact seller. By clicking on that link, you will be redirected to the ad on the originating website. On that page you can find the seller’s contact information.

4. I want to buy a car found on your website.

All information about the conditions for purchase can be found on the originating website, by clicking on the link Contact seller on the page with the detailed description of your chosen car. OOYYO does not own the cars in the listings and therefore takes no part in the sale.

5. I want to buy a car found on your website and import it to another country. Where can I find information about exporting?

OOYYO is not responsible for the sale, purchase, export or import of cars from its listings. For more information on purchase and export conditions, please contact the seller whose information can be found by clicking on the Contact seller link on the page with the detailed description of the chosen car.

6. I found a car I want to buy, but the ad has been removed from/has expired on the originating website.

Despite the fact that data on OOYYO is updated on a daily basis, the selection of cars changes from hour to hour. The car you found has been sold (or removed for some other reason) subsequent to OOYYO collecting the information on the car in question.

7. Why is the price of a car in some cases different on OOYYO from the price on the originating website?

The car dealer or owner has changed the price after OOYYO collected the information from that website.

8. I want to buy a car abroad but I don’t speak the language of that country.

OOYYO allows you to search for cars in 26 countries in the language of your choice from a range of 26 languages. First, choose the desired language from the drop-down menu in the upper right corner and then the search country by selecting the desired country in the search box on the homepage or the results page.

9. I have a suggestion, idea or proposal for cooperation.

If you haven’t found the answer to your question here, or you have a proposal for cooperation with OOYYO, please contact us by filling out the form on the Contact us page.

BENEFITS OF AN XML FEED! Why an XML feed?

Improve the visibility of your ads on OOYYO

We would like to explain the way in which we generate data from your site, and the benefits of XML feeds.

Our crawling program indexes your site page by page, beginning with the home page, or using your sitemap.xml if you have one, respecting the rules in your robots.txt file in doing so.

A more accurate and practical way of doing this is by using XML feeds. By sending us data on all your vehicles via XML feeds means:

  • 100% fresh data with no expired links
  • 100% accurate data without malformed URLs and similar problems that are possible when a site is indexed manually.

Since OOYYO's algorithm favours the freshest results with the cleanest data, if you use XML feeds, vehicles from your site will appear more often than other results.
