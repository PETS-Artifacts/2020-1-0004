Cookies

This information enables us to become more familiar with which customers visit the Website, how often they visit, and what parts of the Website they visit most often.

Cookies are small files that are placed on your browser or the hard drive of your computer by the Website and are used for automatic data collection. You can find more information about the individual cookies we use and the purposes for which we use them in the table below:

Cookie Category Purpose of Cookies
Strictly Necessary This is a session cookie. This cookie identifies who the user is, the number of items in the shopping cart, the preferred language, and the preferred shipping option.
Targeting If you access this Website via the link located in the Mini-Circuits newsletter, then these cookies will track the pages that you visit on this Website. These cookies will expire after thirty (30) days of no activity on this Website.

You may set your browser to automatically accept cookies or automatically refuse cookies. You may also set your browser to warn you when receiving a cookie, thereby giving you the choice to accept or refuse it. If you choose to refuse cookies, you may have to manually input user IDs and passwords to access certain information on the Website, and some pages may not fully function.

Google Analytics
Embedded into each page of the Website is a Google Analytics code. This code creates a report of user statistics such as the Internet Protocol address, time, date and other usage data. This report is sent to Google to track and examine the use of the Website and prepare reports on its activities. For further information, please visit www.google.com/policies/privacy/partners/.

To opt out of being tracked by Google Analytics across all websites, please visit http://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.

Do Not Track Signals
Some browsers have incorporated Do Not Track features that can send a signal to the websites you visit indicating you do not wish to be tracked. Mini-Circuits does not currently respond to Do Not Track signals at this time.
