Online Games Corp. Privacy Policy

Online Games Corp. is committed to protecting the privacy of its users. Currently no personally identifiable information about you is collected at all. The only data collected is used for statistical purposes and keep track of the traffic generated by the site.

Third parties may be placing and reading cookies on your browser, or using web beacons to collect information, in the course of ads being served on the website. You may opt out of cookie usage by adjusting the settings of your web browser, although that may limit some of the functionality of the site.

Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on this site. Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to our users based on their visit to this site and other sites on the Internet. Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy.
