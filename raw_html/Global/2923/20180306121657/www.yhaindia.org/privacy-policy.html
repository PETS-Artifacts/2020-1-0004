PRIVACY POLICY

We know that you care how information about you is used. We are committed to protecting your privacy. We will only use the information that we collect about you lawfully. The information about you is collected IF YOU MAKE A BOOKING and includes your: name, e-mail address, credit/debit card details.

The software we use encrypts all your personal information so that it cannot be read as it travels over the Internet.

You can check that the information we hold about you is accurate and up-to-date by contacting us. If you find anything is inaccurate we will delete or amend the error promptly. We will never collect sensitive information about you without your explicit consent.

We will not e-mail you in the future unless you have given us your consent.

LEGAL REQUESTS

We cooperate with law enforcement inquiries, as well as other third parties to enforce law and order, intellectual property rights, fraud and other rights, to help protect you and the community from crime. Therefore, in response to a verified request by law enforcement or other government officials and other criminal investigation or alleged illegal activity, we can (and you authorize us to) disclose your name, state, telephone number, e-mail address/User ID history, fraud complaints, and bidding and listing without a subpoena. Without limiting the above, in an effort to respect your privacy and our commitment to keep the community free from bad actors, we will not otherwise disclose your personal information to enforcement or to other government officials without a subpoena, court order or substantially adhered to the procedure, except when we believe in good faith that the disclosure of information is necessary and may cause imminent physical harm or financial loss; or report suspected illegal activity. Further, we are authorized by you to disclose your name/street address/city, state/zip code/country, phone number and company name to Intellectual Property right’s owners under confidentiality agreement, at the sole discretion believed necessary or appropriate in connection with an investigation of fraud property infringement, piracy, or other unlawful activity. Due to the existing regulatory environment, we cannot ensure that all of your private communication and other personal information will never be disclosed in ways not otherwise described in this Privacy Agreement. By way of example (without limiting the foregoing), we may be forced to disclose personal information to government or third parties under certain circumstances, third parties may unlawfully intercept transmissions or private communications, or users may abuse or misuse your personal information they collect from the Site. Therefore, although we use International (industry) standard practices to protect your identity we do not promise, and you should not expect, that your personal information or private communication would always remain private.
