PART I. INFORMATION COLLECTION

GirlsAskGuys collects information through our website at several points. We may collect the following information about primary visitors: email address, gender, date of birth, zip code and country.

We may collect the following general data that is not personally identifiable information user name and password. We collect demographic data in order to refine local search criteria when you interact with other members. We collect this information through registration forms. The information is collected voluntarily. To receive our products and services, an email address, a username, date of birth zip code and country are requested to avoid fraudulent activity, prevent abuse of site and its contents and maintain records for your use. Other information we request is optional.

We do employ cookies. A cookie is a small text file that our Web server places on a user’s computer hard drive to be a unique identifier. Cookies enable GirlsAskGuys to track usage patterns and deliver customized content to users. Our cookies do not have an expiration date. Our cookies do not collect personally identifiable information. We also use session ID cookies to confirm that users are logged in. These cookies terminate once the user closes the browser. By default, we use a persistent cookie that stores your login ID (but not your password) to make it easier for you to login when you come back to GirlsAskGuys. You can remove or block this cookie using the settings in your browser.

When you enter GirlsAskGuys, we collect your browser type and IP address. This information is gathered for all GirlsAskGuys users. We also collect website use information as you interact with our website, including but not limited to asking and/or answering questions, sending or receiving instant messages, writing editorials, articles and comments. You post this User Content, as defined in the GirlsAskGuys Terms of Use, on the website at your own risk. Although we allow you to set your privacy options that limit access to your pages, questions and answers, please be aware that no security measures are perfect or impenetrable. We cannot control the actions of other Users with whom you may choose to disclose your pages and information. Therefore, we cannot and do not guarantee that User Content you post on the website will not be viewed by unauthorized persons. We are not responsible for circumvention of any privacy settings or security measures contained on the website. You understand and acknowledge that, even after removal, copies of User Content may remain viewable in cached and archived pages or if other Users have copied or stored your User Content.

If you choose to use our “email a question” service to send a friend a question, we will ask you for your name and your friend's email address. We will automatically send your friend a one-time email, which includes the question. GirlsAskGuys stores this information to send this one-time email, to track the success of our referral program. Your friend may contact us at privacy@girlsaskguys.com to request that we remove this information from our database.

Any improper collection or misuse of information provided on GirlsAskGuys is a violation of the GirlsAskGuys Terms of Use and should be reported to privacy@girlsaskguys.com.
