Privacy

Gooya.com collects only aggregate information
pertaining to visitors to its network of sites.
Gooya.com uses this information to help
improve its web site and services, and to customize
the content of its pages for its regular viewers.

Gooya.com does not sell or commercially make available
specific information about its users or members other
than in aggregate form, except in cooperation with law
enforcement bodies in regards to
violations of applicable laws.

Gooya.com maintains a database of member information
which is used only for internal purposes such as
advertising & technical support.

Any Gooya.com member may remove their information from
our datbase at any time by sending an email to:
info @ gooya . com
with the word "remove" in the subject line.
