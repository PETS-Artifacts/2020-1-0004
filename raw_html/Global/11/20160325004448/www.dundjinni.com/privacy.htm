Privacy Statement

Your privacy is important to you – and it is also important to us.

So here’s how our lawyers explain it:

This Privacy Policy does not govern privacy practices associated with offline activities. Dundjinni Enterprises, Inc. reserves the right to change the Privacy Policy from time to time at its sole discretion. Except as set forth in this Privacy Policy or the Terms of Use, your personal information will be deemed to be confidential. Personal information is defined as any information that identifies or can be used to identify, contact, or locate the person to whom such information pertains. Any non-personal information or material sent to Dundjinni Enterprises, Inc. will generally be deemed to not be confidential. You understand and agree that we may disclose information about you if we have a good faith belief that we are required to do so by law or legal process, to respond to claims, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Dundjinni Enterprises, Inc. or others.

To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, Dundjinni Enterprises, Inc. has appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the personal information collected online. Protecting the privacy of the very young is especially important. For that reason, Dundjinni Enterprises, Inc. never collects or maintains information at our web site from those we actually know are under 13, and no part of the web site is structured to attract anyone under 13.

If, at the time you provide personal information to Dundjinni Enterprises, Inc., you are given the opportunity to limit how such information will be used to communicate with you, either by Dundjinni Enterprises, Inc. or by third parties, Dundjinni Enterprises, Inc. will not change your preference in this regard without your express consent. However, if Dundjinni Enterprises, Inc. sells assets (or the assets of a division or subsidiary) to another entity, or Dundjinni Enterprises, Inc. (or a division or subsidiary) is acquired by, or merged with, another entity, Dundjinni Enterprises, Inc. may provide to such entity customer information that is related to that part of our business that was sold to or merged with the other entity without your consent.

Dundjinni Enterprises, Inc. collects your personal information in order to record and support your participation in the activities you select. Personal information resides on a secure server. We use the personal information to complete an order.

Dundjinni Enterprises, Inc. uses "cookies" to track your visit. A cookie is a small amount of data that is transferred to your browser by a Internet Web server and can only be read by the server that gave it to you. It functions as your identification card, and enables Dundjinni Enterprises, Inc. to record your passwords, purchases, and other use preferences. Dundjinni Enterprises, Inc. does not generally use the IP/Internet address of your computer to identify you personally unless you expressly provide consent to remember or store your password.

Dundjinni Enterprises, Inc. may also collect information about the use of the Site, such as the types of services used and how many users we receive daily. This information is collected in aggregate form, without identifying any user individually. Dundjinni Enterprises, Inc. may use this aggregate, non-identifying statistical data for statistical analysis, marketing, or similar promotional purposes.

The Dundjinni Enterprises, Inc. Web Site contains links to other Internet sites at which personal information is collected. In such instances, unless expressly provided to the contrary, the collection and use of your personal information will be governed by the privacy policy applicable to that site. Dundjinni Enterprises, Inc. is not responsible for the content or privacy practices of non-Dundjinni Enterprises, Inc. Web sites to which there may be a link so the User should review the privacy policy of such Internet sites before using them.

You can engage in many activities without providing any personal information. Other activities may require you to provide personal information. Dundjinni Enterprises, Inc. or third parties authorized by Dundjinni Enterprises, Inc. may also collect certain technical and routing information about your computer to facilitate your use and track patterns. This may include collecting environmental variable data, such as browser type, operating system, CPU speed, and the Internet Protocol ("IP") address of your computer.

When you submit personal information to Dundjinni Enterprises, Inc., you understand and agree that this information may be transferred across national boundaries and may be stored and processed in any of the countries in which Dundjinni Enterprises, Inc. and its affiliates, subsidiaries, or contractors maintain offices, including, without limitation, the United States.
