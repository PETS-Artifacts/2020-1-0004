Privacy Policy

This information rests in our systems for 1-2 days, before it is deleted. There are no other copies of this information anywhere else, so the deletion is definite.

These companies may use information (not including your name, address email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. These companies typically use a cookie or third party web beacon to collect this information. To learn more about this behavioral advertising practice or to opt-out of this type of advertising, you can visit www.networkadvertising.org.
