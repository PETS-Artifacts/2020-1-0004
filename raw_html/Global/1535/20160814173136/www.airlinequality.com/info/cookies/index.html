Like most websites, this site uses cookies. Cookies are small text files stored on your computer by your browser. They’re used for many things, such as remembering whether you’ve visited the site before, or to help us work out how many new website visitors we get each month.



How we use cookies?

By using this website www.airlinequality.com, you agree that we can store and access cookies, IP addresses and use other methods in order to collect website usage data, and improve your online experience of our website. This can include information about your use of the website, the browser and device type you are accessing it from and where you are in the world.

When we use cookies?

We use cookies on www.airlinequality.com for:

Technical : Cookies are used for technical reasons to enable the efficient working of www.airlinequality.com.

Ease of use : Cookies are used to enhance the ease of site use for www.airlinequality.com customers.

Tracking : Cookies are used to gather statistics on how visitors use our site, so that we can make improvements to its usability.

Marketing : Cookies are used to allow third parties to display appropriate advertising and to track its effectiveness

How you can opt out of cookies?

For further information about how to refuse cookies, please refer to www.allaboutcookies.org.
