Privacy Policy

The Cleveland Clinic Center for Continuing Education (The Center) respects your privacy. We will not sell or rent your personal information. We will use this data solely to communicate to you information on various continuing medical education (CME) and educational activities that may be of interest and for no other purpose.

There are three ways in which we collect personally identifiable information through our online CME Community on our Website clevelandclinicmeded.com. The first is upon sign-up to the community whereby a user provides a name, address, email address, telephone number, credentials, and areas of interest. This information is stored in a database.

The second way we collect information is when an online user completes an educational activity and applies for CME credit. This information is stored in a database so that the Center may track the number of online CME participants who wish to obtain CME credit. This record-keeping is required by the Accreditation Council for Continuing Medical Education.

The third way we collect personally identifiable information is during enrollment for our email update service. Users are asked to provide contact information in order to receive alerts relating to online CME activities, including surveys so that the Center can meet the greatest need and interest of the community. An opt-out form enables news alert subscribers to unsubscribe at any time.

We do not distribute unsolicited emails, nor permit use of this subscriber data by other institutions or other third-party organizations.

Usage Information - Collection

We analyze our Site traffic to determine which pages are visited most often and which external websites refer the most visitors to clevelandclinicmeded.com. The Center performs periodic basic demographic analyses.

Usage information is automatically collected by our web server which is hosted by a third-party that your web browser makes available whenever you visit or use our Site, such as the Internet Protocol (IP) address used to connect your computer to the Internet or your computerâ€™s browser type. We may append information provided to us by a third party through the use of audience management tags, also known as cookies. The Site uses session cookies to collect information about the use of the Site.

You may opt out to receive certain electronic communications from us by using the unsubscribe option or other means provided within the communications that you receive. Please note that the unsubscribe feature generally applies on a specific basis and does not provide a means to opt out of receiving all communications from Cleveland Clinic.

About this privacy policy

This is the current privacy policy of the Site. We reserve the right to change this policy at any time. Your continued use of the Site signifies your acceptance of the Privacy Policy. If you do not agree to the terms of the policy, do not access or use our Site and do not provide any information to us. This Site may contain links to other websites that are beyond our control. Other websites to which you link may contain privacy policies that are different from this privacy policy. We encourage you to check the privacy policy of each website you visit before submitting any information to them.
