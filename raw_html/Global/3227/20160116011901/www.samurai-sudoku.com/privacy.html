Our Privacy Policy


Personally Identifiable Information

We do not collect personally identifiable information about the visitors to our website. We never sell your personal information to third parties.

Cookies and Web Beacons

This website does not use cookies, except to store your position when you click Save to save your current samurai sudoku progress. This website does not use web beacons.

We use third party advertisers to support our site. These advertisers may use cookies and/or web beacons when they serve advertisements on our site.

DoubleClick DART cookies

Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on this website. Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads based on your visit to this site and other sites on the Internet. You may opt out of the use of the DART cookie on all sites using this advertising by visiting http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html

Log Files

Log file information is automatically reported by your web browser each time you view a web page. We collect and use the data contained in log files. Log files include information such as your internet protocol address, your internet service provider, your browser type and language, the time you visited our site, and which pages you visited on our site.
