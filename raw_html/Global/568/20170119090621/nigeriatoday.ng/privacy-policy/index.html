At Nigeriatoday.ng, the privacy of our visitors is of extreme importance to us. This privacy policy document outlines the types of personal information is received and collected by Nigeriatoday.ng and how it is used.

Log Files

Like many other Web sites, Nigeriatoday.ng makes use of log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol ( IP ) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider ( ISP ), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user’s movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

Cookies and Web Beacons
Nigeriatoday.ng does not use cookies.
