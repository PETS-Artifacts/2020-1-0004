Scam Alert

Please be aware that we've had reports of customers and non-customers receiving fake Origin bills via email. If you believe you have received one of these emails we strongly recommend not clicking on any links. We also encourage you to report any scams at: www.scamwatch.gov.au. For more information on how to tell a scam from a real Origin message read our Blog article. How do you tell a scam from a real Origin message?
