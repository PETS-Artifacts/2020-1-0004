Again, this information does not include any Personally Identifiable Information about you or allow anyone to identify you individually.

Personally Identifiable Information collected by KDHNews.com may also be used for other purposes, including but not limited to site administration, troubleshooting, processing of e-commerce transactions, administration of sweepstakes and contests, and other communications with you. We will use your information only as permitted by law. In addition, from time to time as we continue to develop our business, we may sell, buy, merge or partner with other companies or businesses. Please note we may not provide you with notice prior to disclosure in such cases.
