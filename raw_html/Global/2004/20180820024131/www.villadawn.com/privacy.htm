Privacy Policy

At VillaDawn.com, we are firmly committed to protecting your privacy. We use the information we collect about you only to complete a rental reservation or create listings for advertising purposes. Below you'll find our privacy statement that discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this Web site, VillaDawn.com.

Your Personal Information
We do not collect personal information, such as e-mail address or telephone number, from you on our site unless you inquire about availability, make an online reservation or advertise with VillaDawn.com, we collect your personal information to answer your inquiries and for billing purposes only.

Security
VillaDawn.com has security measures in place to reasonably protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control.   Our online reservation/payment form is secured by Geotrust.

Children's Guidelines
We at VillaDawn.com adhere to the following concerning information provided by children.

1. NOT to intentionally collect online contact information from children without prior parental consent or parental notification, which will include an opportunity for the parent to prevent use of the information and participation in the activity. Without prior parental consent online information will only be used to respond directly to the child's request and will not be used for other purposes without prior parental consent.
2. NOT to collect personally identifiable off-line contact information without prior parental consent.
3. NOT to distribute to third parties any personally identifiable information without prior parental consent.
4. NOT to give the ability to publicly post or otherwise distribute personally identifiable contact information without prior parental consent.
5. NOT to entice by the prospect of a special game, prize or other activity, to divulge more information than is needed to participate in the activity.

Registration & Order Forms
When you request availability and/or advertising information, we collect your name, e-mail address and phone number. This allows us to respond to your request.

When you submit advertising, we need to know your name, e-mail address, mailing address, phone number, credit card number and its expiration date. This is all done on a secure server or via the telephone.

When you submit a review of a restaurant, we ask for your e-mail address so we can contact you if we have questions about your review. If you are willing to be contacted via e-mail, by other VillaDawn.com users who may want to ask more questions about the restaurant you reviewed, we will include your e-mail address. However, you can choose not to have your e-mail address displayed with your review. We also ask for your name and phone number. This is not published on the Web site and is used only to verify the legitimacy of a review.

We may also use the information we collect to notify you occasionally about new VillaDawn.com services, promotions and special offers we think you'll find valuable. If you choose not to receive such notices, you can send us an email and we will honor your request.

Will VillaDawn.com disclose the information it collects to outside parties?
VillaDawn.com will not, under any circumstances, disclose any information collected to any outside parties.

External Links
This site contains links to other sites. VillaDawn.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.

In Summary
VillaDawn.com is committed to protecting your privacy. We use the information we collect on the site to enhance your visit to our Web site and to occasionally send you special offers and announcements.

Your Consent
By using our Web site, you permit VillaDawn.com to collect and use this information as outlined above. If we change our privacy policy, we will post the changes on this page. You will always be aware of the information we collect, how we intend to use it and under what circumstances we disclose it.

Contacting the Web Site
If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this Web site, send us an email.
