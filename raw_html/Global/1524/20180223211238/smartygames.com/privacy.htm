Privacy

At SmartyGames.com, we are committed to make safe

and quality content that family members of all ages

can enjoy. We recognize, adhere to and applaud the

Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), and

remain active in seeking the best in security measures,

family-safe content, and personal privacy.

No personal information is gathered about our visitors in the general areas of our web site. If you send us an e-mail with a question or comment, or enter one of our contests, we will use your e-mail address only to reply to you, but we will not keep the address or use it again. We do not exclude anyone from our site for not giving us personal information. In the case that we do ask for personal information (like an e-mail address), we will only keep it until that particular activity is complete. We will not share this information with a third party. If we know a visitor’s age is twelve or under, we will collect a parent's permission before we mail any prizes, or collect or share personal information with anyone else. Your family's use of this site is conditioned on your having accepted these terms. You should check back to this policy frequently, since it is subject to change from time to time, and your continued use of the site is conditioned upon your acceptance of any modifications hereto. Our goal is to make this site a fun educational place where families can browse together, and where parents and caregivers are happy to have their children interact, learn and play. If you have any questions or concerns regarding this policy, we're happy to hear them. Please contact us on our online form.
