Privacy Policy

Reserva Conchal Costa Rica and The Westin Golf Resort & Spa, Playa Conchal value your privacy. We want you to be confident in using this site to make your online reservations. We are very much aware of your concerns about the confidentiality of your information and we have no desire or intent to infringe on your privacy.

We will not provide your personal information to anyone else. When you submit your personal information, such as name, address, e-mail address, and telephone number, we will not give or sell this information to any outside company for any use.
