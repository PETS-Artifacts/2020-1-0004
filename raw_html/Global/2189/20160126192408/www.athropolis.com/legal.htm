Children's Privacy:

Protecting the privacy of the young is especially important. Therefore, we never solicit, collect or maintain personal information at our web site from those we actually know are under 13. See Submissions for Publication

Browser information collected on the web site:

We analyze our web site logs to constantly improve both the site and the value of the materials available on the site. Our web site logs are not personally identifiable, and we make no attempt to link them with the individuals that actually browse the site.

Resale or disclosure of information to third parties:

We do not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease any personal information collected at our site, including membership forms, mailing addresses or e-mail lists.

Privacy of our e-mail lists:

We maintain several e-mail lists to keep our members, the press, and interested members of the general public informed about breaking events. Individuals must agree to join our mailing lists by signing up through a form on our web site or by participating in our retail offers.

We do not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease the addresses on our list to anyone. The e-mail addresses of our list subscribers are not divulged to anyone but authorized staff.

Finally, we never use or share the personally identifiable information provided to us online in ways unrelated to the ones described above without also providing you an opportunity to opt-out or otherwise prohibit such unrelated uses.
