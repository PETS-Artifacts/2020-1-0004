User Privacy at GCSE.com

GCSE.com does not collect any personal information about anyone using our site - you can use our site for free, accessing all areas anonymously. Our web servers may log the country from which you are viewing our site, as well as other information such as the type of computer and web browser being used, etc.

Guestbook

If you communicate directly, or indirectly, to us via our Guestbook or email addresses, we may respond to you in due course - particularly if you ask us a question. Other than answering questions, we will not use your details for any other purpose. Personal information left in the Guestbook will, however, remain in the Guestbook if your comment is approved.

Advertising

In addition to custom advertising solutions, we use some third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and would like to know your options in relation to not having this information used by these companies, click here.
