Collection of Data

Blues for Peace does not collect Internet User identity or contact information from Internet Users' visits to Blues for Peace Web Sites. For each visitor to a Blues for Peace Web site, the visitor's computer reaches us via a particular IP, (Internet Protocol), address. Blues for Peace's servers automatically collect this IP address.

An Internet User's browser sets Cookies (when enabled) that contain small amounts of data on the Internet User's computer after a visit to any web site. Cookies assist Blues for Peace in tracking Internet Users' activities on the Blues for Peace website. The Cookies themselves tell Blues for Peace nothing about the Internet User personally.

No information is obtained from the Internet User or Internet User's computer to generate the unique identifying number of the Internet User. Blues for Peace's servers automatically generate the unique number. None of Blues for Peace's Cookies are used to perform "User Profiling".

Blues for Peace does receive and record e-mail addresses of Internet Users, only in circumstances where the Internet User has voluntarily provided its identity and contact information. This e-mail address does not enable Blues for Peace to personally identify an Internet User.

Use and Disclosure of Data

Blues for Peace does not sell or rent personally identifying information. Blues for Peace does not disclose personally identifying information to anyone that Blues for Peace is not authorized to do so.

Blues for Peace reserves the right to release information on Internet Users if compelled to do so by proper judicial or governmental authorities. Disclosure shall be limited to only to those that Blues for Peace is compelled to disclose to. At present, all information processing occurs in the United States.

Security Measures

Blues for Peace employs industry standard security measures to ensure the security of all data. Access to Blues for Peace customer data is accessible via password protection to designated Blues for Peace employees who require system level access in order to perform their jobs.

Spam

Blues for Peace does not intentionally send unsolicited e-mail messages that could legitimately be construed as spam.

COPPA

Blues for Peace complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act of 1998. Advertisers are also obligated to comply with COPPA.

If you inform Blues for Peace that Blues for Peace has collected or received information from a child under 18, Blues for Peace will delete such information immediately from its databases. No Blues for Peace Web Site is specifically directed to children under 18. Blues for Peace shall not knowingly distribute to third parties any personally identifiable information erroneously collected from children under 18.

Deactivating - deleting from database.

Please contact info@bluesforpeace.com if you no longer wish to be listed in the Blues for Peace database. If Blues for Peace has such information, Blues for Peace shall not retain your name and/or e-mail address any longer than is necessary to perform the services that you requested from Blues for Peace.

Policy Changes

Blues for Peace may find it necessary or be legally obligated to update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted at this site. Except as otherwise required by law, modifications to this Privacy Policy will not affect the privacy of data collected by Blues for Peace prior to the effective date of the policy change.
