Privacy Policy

All searches on Index UAE are recorded.

Information collected includes the browser used, IP address from which our site is accessed, search terms used etc.

This information is used merely to track how effective we are in providing the information that users seek and to improve our search software. It is not traced back to individual users, and typically is not stored for more than a few weeks.

Google's interest-based advertising allows advertisers to show ads based on a user's previous interactions with them, such as visits to advertiser website and also to reach users based on their interests (e.g. "sports enthusiast").
