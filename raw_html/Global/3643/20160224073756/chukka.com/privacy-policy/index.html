    General

    Chukka Caribbean Adventures only collects personally identifiable information on a voluntary basis. Chukka Caribbean Adventures does not require that you provide this information to gain access to the websites, and it does not require you to disclose more information than is reasonably needed to participate in an activity on the websites. Chukka Caribbean Adventures collects no identifiable information primarily to e-mail updates and news, for entry into promotions and to notify subsequent winners, to fulfill brochure requests etc. via regular mail, and to respond to your questions or concerns. Chukka Caribbean Adventures does not sell, transfer or disclose any information gathered at our websites to anyone outside. You may unsubscribe from emails by simply clicking on the unsubscribe link found at the top and bottom of the email.

    Cookies on Chukka

    A cookie is a small text file stored on a user’s hard drive. Depending on how you have configured your browser, when you visit our site we use cookies. Cookies are required to shop on our site. We use cookies to assist with anonymous site traffic analysis, which includes tracking the time/date of visits, pages viewed, and referring URLs.

    There are simple ways to either refuse all cookies, or accept them each time they are offered. Consult your browser’s help files for assistance on changing cookie settings or removing cookie files.
